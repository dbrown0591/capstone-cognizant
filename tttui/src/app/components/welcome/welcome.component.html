Tic- Tac- Toe
Str ate gies Win: If the player has two in a row, they can place a third to get three in a row. Block: If the opponent has two in a row, the player must play the third themselves to block the opponent. Fork: Create an opportunity where the player has two ways to win (two non-blocked lines of 2). Blocking an opponent's fork: If there is only one possible fork for the opponent, the player should block it.
